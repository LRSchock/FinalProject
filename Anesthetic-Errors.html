Whenever you have surgery anesthesia is required. Although anesthesia has many benefits, when done improperly the results can be devastating causing brain damage or even death.

Anesthesiologists can err in many ways and it is the job of the medical malpractice attorney to determine whether or not negligence was involved. Therefore, you should call us if you think that you or a family member have suffered as a result of anesthesia malpractice. Please call our toll free number at 1-(888) 878-3342 today.
