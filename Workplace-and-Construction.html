Workplace Construction Accidents

New York Personal Injury Lawyers - Workplace and Construction Accidents



Construction sites pose many dangers to workers, contractors, and bystanders who are in the area. Construction workers are exposed to a wider variety of hazards and face a greater risk of work related injuries and fatalities than employees than those in any other industry.

Due to the inherent risks construction workers face, a unique and complicated set of labor law statues govern construction sites and construction related accidents. The applicable law provides for liability against owners and contractors for injuries caused by falls from heights, even where no acts of negligence were committed. In most instances, Federal and State laws will both apply. If you were injured on the job, it is critical that you find an experienced attorney with knowledge of all of the relevant issues and laws immediately to help process your case and protect your rights.

While workers’ compensation provides workers injured on the job with benefits, including payments based on lost wages, and total or partial disability, this is usually not enough compensation for the victim.  Our construction accident lawyers fight hard for construction and workplace accident victims, winning settlements that compensate for medical expenses, lost income and pain and suffering.  Our experienced attorneys will fight to get you the compensation you are entitled to above and beyond workman’s compensation.

At the Law Offices of Ross, Legan, Rosenberg, Zelen & Flaks, LLP, we have attorneys and staff who are highly experienced in construction injury claims. Our attorneys know how to thoroughly investigate the case, secure relevant evidence, and help you get through this often complicated, confusing and stressful period of your life, as you recover from your injuries. In our years of experience we have handled many construction and other workplace accidents including:

  • Fall from ladders
  • Fall from roofs or other heights
  • Scaffolding collapses and falls
  • Collapsed or falling walls, structures and ditches
  • Defective equipment
  • Electrocution
  • Fire/Explosions
  • Welding accidents
  • Crane collapses
  • Window washing related falls
  • Taxi, Limo and Bus Driver Accidents
  • School, Police, Fire and City Employee Accidents

Our firm will handle every aspect of the case from talking to witnesses, to getting information from your employer, to investigating the worksite, the general contractor, and owner.
