Wills, Trusts & Estates

New York City Estate Planning Lawyer



The Law Offices of Ross, Legan, Rosenberg, Zelen & Flaks, LLP offers a wide range of comprehensive estate planning services, including the creation and execution of wills, trusts, and estates. We strongly believe that every adult should have a properly executed estate plan, regardless of age, marital status, or amount of assets. Our New York City estate planning lawyers are committed to helping clients plan their estates in the most efficient and cost-effective manner possible.

Some of the estate planning services we offer are:

  • Comprehensive Estate Plans – includes the creation of a will, trusts, power of attorney, health care proxy, and beneficiary designation.
  • Living Trust – provides lifetime and after-death asset management to avoid probate.
  • Supplemental Needs Trust – designed to benefit a person with a mental or physical disability.
  • Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust (ILIT) – takes ownership of a life insurance policy to reduce estate tax liability.
  • Strategies to Reduce or Eliminate Estate Tax – crucial for individuals whose estate will be taxable at the state and/or federal level.
  • Review and Revision of Wills and Estate Planning Documents – ensure documents are up-to-date with recent changes in the law and tax code.

In addition to estate planning, we also provide expertise in the following related areas of practice:

  • Estate Administration
  • Trust Administration
  • Estate Litigation

Our New York City estate planning lawyers bring a unique and valuable set of skills to the legal services that we offer. We treat every client as a unique individual and always tailor our legal services to meet each client’s unique circumstances.

As your New York City wills, trusts and estates lawyers, we will provide you with the time and dedication that you and your loved ones deserve. With law offices conveniently located in Manhattan and Queens, New York and Hackensack, New Jersey, our lawyers are available to assist clients with their estate planning matters throughout the New York metro area.
