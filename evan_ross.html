Evan Ross, born in 1977, graduated from the University of Miami School of Law in 2002. After receiving his law degree, Mr. Ross furthered his legal education at the University of Miami School of Law where he received a Master’s Degree “LL.M” in Estate Planning in 2003. Prior to receiving his Masters Degree, Mr. Ross was the recipient of the prestigious Phillip E. Heckerling Scholarship award. Mr. Ross was admitted to the practice of law in the State of Florida in 2002, and in the State of New Jersey in 2003, and in the State of New York in 2004. Mr. Ross is also admitted to the United States District Courts for the Southern and Eastern Districts of New York. Before his admission to these bars, Mr. Ross was employed in the year 2000 as a judicial intern by the Honorable Barry L. Garber, of the United States District Court for the Southern District of Florida. After practicing Estate Planning and probate law in Florida for several years, Mr. Ross joined the firm of Ross, Legan, Rosenberg, Zelen & Flaks. Today, Mr. Ross has expanded his areas of practice to include Estate Planning, Wills, Trusts, Estates, Probate, Estate Litigation, Pre-Nuptial and Post-Nuptial Agreements. Mr. Ross also litigates on behalf of accident victims from New York, New Jersey and Florida. Most recently, Mr. Ross became Outside Legal Counsel to a Financial Services Corporation in Florida as well.
